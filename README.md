# TASK-2 : Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence

## Objective

This task focuses on uncovering patterns, trends, and relationships within the dataset through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), SQL-based business analysis, correlation studies, and dashboard development to support business decision-making.

---

# Dataset Overview

The dataset contains Superstore sales transaction details including:

* Customer Information
* Product Information
* Sales Details
* Shipping Details
* Profit & Discount Information

Total Records: **9994 Rows**
Total Columns: **21 Columns**

---

# Operations Performed

## Descriptive Statistics & Univariate Analysis

Performed statistical analysis and visual exploration of the dataset:

* Summary Statistics
* Sales Distribution Analysis
* Profit Distribution Analysis
* Category Distribution Analysis
* Revenue Analysis

---

## SQL Business Questions

Answered business-focused questions using SQL queries:

* Top 5 Products by Revenue
* Top 5 Customers by Revenue
* Region-wise Profit Analysis
* Category-wise Revenue Analysis
* Average Discount Analysis

---

## Multivariate Analysis & Correlation

Performed advanced analysis to identify relationships between variables:

* Correlation Matrix Analysis
* Correlation Heatmap
* Sales vs Profit Analysis
* Pair Plot Analysis
* Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis

---

## Dashboard Development

Created a static business dashboard containing:

### Dashboard KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Average Discount

### Dashboard Visualizations

* Sales by Region
* Profit by Category
* Monthly Revenue Trend
* Top 10 Products by Revenue

---

# Key Business Insights

* West Region generated the highest profit.
* Technology category generated the highest revenue.
* Higher discounts negatively impacted profitability.
* A small number of products contributed significantly to total sales.
* Sales and Profit showed a positive correlation.
